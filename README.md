# Violets-USB-Hub
A cool little pcb usb hub thats also a keychain!
<img width="3832" height="4016" alt="e14d643b036a42dcbcabad9dc9a3bc18_T" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/47cd5701-3897-4d88-a406-7762f30915ae" />

## What?
A usb hub, usb 3.0 with 3 usb-a inputs 1 usb-c input and 1 usb c input,all on one hexagon shaped decorated pcb that even has a place to attach a keychain!

## Why?
1. Its a very cool starting project into pcb design, and its just really cool to have, for your phone, for the annoying laptop by a certain fruit logo company that has no ports or whatever you wanna plug it in!
2. I really needed a usb hub

## How do i use it?
Plug the upper usb-c port next to the key hole into your laptop, phone etc. and plug your usb devices into the others!!
